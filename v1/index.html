Introduction

Fettuccine Alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish made with fettuccine pasta, butter, and Parmesan cheese.

This recipe is a family favorite that has been passed down through generations.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions.
  2. Melt butter in a pan.
  3. Add cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add cheese and stir until melted.
  5. Combine sauce with pasta.
  6. Serve immediately.
